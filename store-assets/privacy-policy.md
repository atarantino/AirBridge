# AirBridge Video Sync Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 18, 2026

AirBridge Video Sync does not collect, retain, sell, share, or transmit personal information or browsing data to the developer or any third party.

The extension processes HTML5 video frames locally in the browser to delay the visible picture. Captured frames are held temporarily in a bounded in-memory queue and are discarded after display, when playback state changes, or when synchronization is disabled. Frames never leave the user's device.

The extension stores only the user's per-site enable preference and configured delay value using the browser's synchronized extension storage. If browser synchronization is enabled, the browser vendor may synchronize those preferences through the user's browser account under the vendor's own privacy terms.

The extension does not use analytics, advertising, tracking, cookies, remote code, or native messaging.

The use of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. Website content and browsing activity are used only to provide the extension's user-facing video synchronization feature and are never sold, transferred for advertising, or made available for human review.
